Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Restaurant Ordering System ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, password, hotel name, address, and payment information.

Order Data: We collect information about orders placed through your restaurant, including items ordered, quantities, prices, and timestamps.

Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you interact with our service, including logs, performance data, and user behavior patterns.

Payment Information: Payment data is collected and processed through Razorpay, our secure payment partner. We do not store your full credit card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • To process your subscription and payments
  • To send you service-related announcements
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To generate reports and analytics for your business
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activities
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you close your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

Historical order data may be retained for reporting and analytics purposes even after subscription ends, for up to 7 days.

6. Data Isolation

Your data is completely isolated from other restaurants using our platform. We use strict access controls and database filtering to ensure that you can only access your own data. No cross-hotel data sharing occurs.

7. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services such as Razorpay for payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain communications
  • Request a data portability report

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@restaurantsystem.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date above.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:
